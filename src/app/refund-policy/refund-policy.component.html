Refund Policy

Understand our refund policy at Logs, our inventory management solution.

Last Updated: 26th January, 2025

At Logs, we strive to provide quality inventory management solutions. If you are unsatisfied with your subscription or encounter issues, please review our refund policy below:

Refunds may be considered under the following conditions:
  • You experienced technical issues preventing access to the Software for a prolonged period.
  • You were charged incorrectly or for a subscription you did not authorize.

Refunds will not be issued for:
  • Change of mind after subscription purchase.
  • Partial use of subscription services.
  • Failure to cancel the subscription before the next billing cycle.

To request a refund:
  • Submit a written request via email to support@logs.ng.
  • Include your account details, payment receipt, and a description of the issue.
  • Refund requests must be made within 14 days of the billing date.

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after approval.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the subscription.

For refund-related inquiries, please contact us at support@logs.ng.
